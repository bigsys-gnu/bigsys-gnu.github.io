Big Data System Software Lab

We design and build systems that efficiently and reliably process and store large volumes of data in terms of performance, reliability, power, and cost. We are interested in the broad area of system software including operating systems, storage systems, concurrency, and machine learning for systems. All research and study in our lab will begin with your interests, curiosity and self-motivation.

We are looking for undergraduate and graduate students who are interested in software, computer system, machine learning or anything related to computer technologies. Feel free to ping me by email or drop by my office If you are interested.

Research Interests
  • Operating Systems
  • Storage System
  • Concurrency for Many-core Machine
  • Artificial Intelligence Systems
